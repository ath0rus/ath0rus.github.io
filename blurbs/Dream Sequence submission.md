
This piece is my submission to the **Pwnisher “Dream Sequence” 3D Challenge**. From initial block out to final render, this project took about a month to complete, roughly **250 hours** of time working on the scene, plus **2.5 hours of final rendering**.

Most of that time went into building out the set, lighting, making assets and materials from various sources work. I pushed myself to really focus on the layout and general composition and making the animations myself, in the process learning alot about animation and rigging

### Workflow & Process

The scene was built and assembled in **Blender** using a mix of pre made assets and custom made assets. A large part of the workflow involved:

- Scene blockout and camera composition
- Making sure custom materials fitted into the scene
- Optimization for final render
### Assets & Resources

This project incorporates assets and resources from:

- KitBash3D
- BigMediumSmall
- Pixabay
- AmbientCG
- Various Blender plugins and asset packs
- Several custom built assets

Huge thanks to **Ryan King Art** for his sushi texture tutorials, they were incredibly helpful in achieving believable surface detail and realism for key elements in the scene.

### Technical Notes

- Software: Blender
- Render Time: ~2.5 hours
- Total Production Time: ~250 hours

This project pushed my knowledge of 3D and helped me learn alot. There was lots of late nights and weekends spent on this scene but it was a great opportunity to expand my scene building and material skills under a time constraint.
